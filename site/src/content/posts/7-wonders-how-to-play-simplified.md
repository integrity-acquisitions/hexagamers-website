## The Big Picture

7 Wonders is a **card drafting** game. Each player holds a hand of cards, picks one to keep, then passes the rest to their neighbour. This happens simultaneously. Over three ages, you build a tableau of cards that generate Victory Points in various ways. Highest VP total wins.

## Setup

<div class="steps">
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">1</span><div>Each player picks or is randomly dealt a <strong>Wonder board</strong>. Each Wonder has two sides — choose based on preference or experience level.</div></div>
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">2</span><div>Pull out only the Age I cards (marked with a Roman numeral I on the back). Set aside Age II and III cards for later rounds.</div></div>
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">3</span><div>Deal the Age I cards to all players. The number dealt varies by player count — check the rulebook.</div></div>
</div>

## How a Round Works

<div class="steps">
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">1</span><div><strong>All players simultaneously</strong> choose one card from their hand and place it face-down.</div></div>
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">2</span><div>All chosen cards are revealed at once and placed in front of each player's tableau.</div></div>
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">3</span><div>Pass remaining cards to your neighbour: <strong>clockwise in Age I, counter-clockwise in Age II, clockwise again in Age III</strong>.</div></div>
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">4</span><div>Repeat until 2 cards remain in each hand. Those last 2 cards are discarded.</div></div>
  <div class="step"><span class="step-num">5</span><div>At end of each Age, compare army values with both neighbours (see Military below).</div></div>
</div>

## Card Types

<div class="rule-cards">
  <div class="rule-card">
    <p class="rule-num">RESOURCE (Brown/Grey)</p>
    <p class="rule-title">Building blocks</p>
    <p>Generate raw or refined materials. Required to build most other cards. Only available in Age I and II — grab them early.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="rule-card">
    <p class="rule-num">BUILDING (Blue)</p>
    <p class="rule-title">Direct Victory Points</p>
    <p>Immediately add VP to your total at end of game. Simplest scoring path.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="rule-card">
    <p class="rule-num">MILITARY (Red)</p>
    <p class="rule-title">Army comparison</p>
    <p>Compare your army value to neighbours at end of each Age. Win = +VP (increases per age). Lose = −1 VP.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="rule-card">
    <p class="rule-num">SCIENCE (Green)</p>
    <p class="rule-title">Set and collect</p>
    <p>Score VPs for having all 3 different science symbols (7 pts) or multiples of one symbol (number squared).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="rule-card">
    <p class="rule-num">MARKET (Yellow)</p>
    <p class="rule-title">Commerce</p>
    <p>Reduce cost of buying from neighbours, generate coins, or provide other economic benefits.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="rule-card">
    <p class="rule-num">GUILD (Purple)</p>
    <p class="rule-title">End-game bonuses</p>
    <p>Score VP based on your cards, neighbours' cards, or other game state. Only appear in Age III.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Buying a Card

Check the **top-left corner** of any card for its cost. Some are free. Most require resources or coins.

If you can't afford a card, you have three options:
- Buy missing resources from a neighbour for 2 coins each
- Discard the card to receive 3 coins
- Build the next stage of your Wonder (place the card face-down under it)

## Military Scoring

At the end of each Age, compare your military shield count to the person on each side of you:
- **More shields:** +1 VP (Age I), +3 VP (Age II), +5 VP (Age III)
- **Fewer shields:** −1 VP per loss, any age

## End Game

After Age III, count all VP from: buildings, science sets, guilds, military outcomes, Wonders built, and coins (every 3 coins = 1 VP). Highest total wins.

<div class="faq">
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Can I buy from a neighbour I'm not adjacent to?</summary>
    <p>No — you can only buy resources from the players directly to your left and right.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Can I build multiple stages of my Wonder in one game?</summary>
    <p>Yes — each stage requires a specific payment, and you place any card face-down to build it. Most Wonders have 3 stages.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>What happens to the last 2 cards in each hand?</summary>
    <p>They are discarded face-down at the end of each Age. Neither player uses them.</p>
  </details>
</div>
