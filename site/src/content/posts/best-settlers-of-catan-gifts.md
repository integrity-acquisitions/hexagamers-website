[Settlers of Catan (Catan)](http://hexagamers.com/catan-review) is one of the best-known and best-loved board games in circulation today. For many, it was and remains the [gateway game](http://hexagamers.com/best-gateway-board-games-for-beginners-with-reviews): the perfect intro to how board games have evolved, to strategy, to expansions, and the immense world where there's something for everyone.

Catan has a huge following, and retailers, small businesses and entrepreneurs have jumped in the mix, making and selling gifts suitable for any gamer or Catan lover you know. From coasters to clothes to upgraded boards, there is something for everyone on your list. We've captured some of our favourite Catan gifts below, but trust us, the gifts and possibilities are endless.
